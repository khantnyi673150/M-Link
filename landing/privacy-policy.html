Privacy Policy

M-Link - Effective date: April 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how M-Link ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the M-Link mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: email address and basic profile information used for sign-in and account management.
  • User Content: shop details, menu details, and images uploaded by merchants.
  • Usage Data: basic technical and usage information necessary for app performance and reliability.

2. How We Use Information

  • To create and manage user accounts.
  • To display shop and menu data in the app.
  • To store and deliver uploaded images.
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and improve app functionality.

3. Services and Third Parties

M-Link uses trusted third-party services for core app functionality:

  • Firebase (Google): authentication and database services.
  • Cloudinary: image hosting and delivery.
  • External map links: when users tap directions, they may be redirected to map providers.

These providers process data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We only share data with service providers required to run the app, or when required by law.

5. Data Retention

We keep data only as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

M-Link is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, update, or delete your personal information. Contact us to request assistance.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
