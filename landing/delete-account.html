Account Deletion Request

M-Link - Last updated: April 22, 2026

This page explains how users can delete their M-Link account and what data is deleted or retained.

How to Delete Your Account (In-App)

  1. Open M-Link and sign in as a merchant.
  2. Go to the merchant dashboard.
  3. Tap Delete Account in the top bar.
  4. Confirm deletion and enter your password.
  5. Your account is deleted immediately after confirmation.

Data Deleted

  • Authentication account (Firebase Auth user).
  • Merchant shop profile data.
  • Merchant menu items linked to that shop profile.

Data Retained

We do not intentionally retain deleted merchant account/profile records after successful deletion. Some technical logs and backups may remain for a limited period for security, legal, and disaster recovery purposes.

Deletion Timeline

Account deletion is processed immediately in the app workflow. Any backup/log expiration follows provider retention policies.
